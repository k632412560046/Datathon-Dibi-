# 📊 Datathon Dibi — E-commerce Analytics

This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to identify the root causes of revenue decline and customer churn. Results show that performance issues stem from inefficient promotion, low retention, and poor resource allocation — not lack of demand.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Promotion is inefficient: heavy discounts do not drive revenue  
- High churn rate (56.8%) and very low retention (1–3%)  
- At Risk customers hold significant value but are being lost  
- Over-reliance on credit card, under-optimized checkout experience  

---
